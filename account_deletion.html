How to Delete Your Data

Last updated: March 11, 2026

To delete your Fuel Smart account and all associated personal data, please send an email request from your registered email address to:

fuelsmartph@gmail.com

Please use "Account Deletion Request" as the subject line. Once we receive your email, we will verify your identity and process the deletion of the following:

  • Your account profile (full name and email).
  • Reason for deletion(optional)

Note: Contributions to fuel station data may be retained in an anonymized format to maintain the accuracy of the crowdsourced map for other users.
