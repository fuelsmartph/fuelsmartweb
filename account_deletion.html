Fuel Smart – Account & Data Deletion Request

Last updated: March 11, 2026 • Contact: fuelsmartph@gmail.com

Web deletion request link (for Play Store compliance)

If you would like to delete your Fuel Smart account and associated data, submit the request using the form on this page. You may also email us at fuelsmartph@gmail.com.

What we delete

  • Your account profile information (such as name/display preferences and optional tip/donation details).
  • Your access to the app will be removed after deletion is processed.
  • Some contribution history may be retained in an anonymized form to protect data integrity and prevent abuse (where applicable).

Processing time

We aim to process deletion requests within a reasonable time after verifying the request.
